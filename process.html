Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Most questions answered here. Can't find yours? Email us directly — we respond within 24 hours.

Can I order multiple sizes in one run?
Yes. You can mix sizes within the same SKU family (e.g., 8 oz, 12 oz, and 16 oz hot cups) to meet the 5,000-unit MOQ across the mix. Each size still requires a minimum of 1,000 units within the mix.
What file format should I send my artwork in?
We prefer vector files — AI or EPS with outlined fonts. PDF and high-resolution PNG/JPG (300 dpi at print size) are also accepted. We'll supply a dieline template once your cup specs are confirmed.
Do you offer sample cups before committing to a full order?
Yes — sample packs are available before you commit to a full order. A small sample fee applies to all sample requests (blank and custom-printed). The full fee is credited against your first production run, so it costs you nothing in the end. Contact us to arrange a sample pack.
How do reorders work once artwork is approved?
We store your approved digital files and print-ready artwork. Reorders can typically ship within 7–10 business days — no new proof needed unless you update the artwork.
Are your cups certified compostable?
Yes. Our entire cup range — both hot and cold — is made from PLA (polylactic acid), a plant-based bioplastic that meets ASTM D6400 and EN 13432 compostability standards. Certification documents available on request.
Do you ship outside Canada and the USA?
Our primary markets are Canada and the United States, with a focus on the West Coast. International inquiries for large-volume orders (50,000+ units) are considered case by case — contact us to discuss.
